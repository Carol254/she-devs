About Us
ABOUT SHE DEVS
She devs community started in January,2023. Our aim is to encouarage more women to get into Tech. Therefore,bridging the gender gap that currently exists in the Tech field. We aim to create a safe space for women to come together and create solutions that could change the world. Our mission is to empower and support women in the tech industry by providing resources, networking opportunities, and a platform to share their experiences and insights. We believe that diversity and inclusivity are essential to creating innovative and impactful technology. Our community consists of women from all backgrounds and levels of experience in the tech industry. Whether you're just starting your career or have been in the field for years, we welcome you to join our community. Through our website, you'll have access to a variety of resources, including articles on industry trends, interviews with successful women in tech, and job postings. We also organize networking events, mentorship programs, and workshops to help our members develop their skills and advance in their careers. Our community is committed to creating a safe and supportive space for women in tech. We encourage our members to share their experiences, offer advice, and support one another. We believe that by working together, we can create a more equitable and diverse tech industry. Thank you for joining our community, and we look forward to connecting with you!
