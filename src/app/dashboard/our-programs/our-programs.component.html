Hackathons


Hackathons are events meant to work on specific problems and are aimed to build viable solutions.

Hackathons allow programmers learn new skills quickly from other professionals. During the hackathons programmers experience new coding languages,techniques,industry tools and leadership skills they have not experienced yet.

Hackathons are important since they increase one's motivation and most importantly networking with different people.

Mentorship


We don't just focus on technical improving technical skiils.

The mentees are guided and impacted with the right insights to help them become better developers.

Bootcamps


Bootcamps are short-term,intensive programs that aim to teach practical and job-ready tech skills.

Bootcamps play a vital role in supporting women in tech since they provide women with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the industry.

These bootcamps offer mentorship opportunities,networking events and career guidance to help women navigate the tech industry and overcome barriers they may face

The bootcamps will involve various technologies,incoporating a variety of tech fields such web development.
